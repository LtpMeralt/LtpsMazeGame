Trigger Help

Trigger blocks do not show in play mode. They run once when something touches or activates them.

Fired By is empty for the player. Put a tag here to make the trigger wait until a block with that tag touches it.

WIN finishes the level instantly.

DIE kills every player.

CAMERA moves the camera to a grid position and zoom over time.

MOVE_TAG moves every block with the matching tag by the X/Y amount you enter.

ROTATE_TAG rotates every block with the matching tag by the angle you enter.

Tip: use tags like doorA or secret1 so triggers stay readable.
